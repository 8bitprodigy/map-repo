======================= Installation ======================== 

This archive contains 3 files.
-FireF.txt    : You are reading it now.
-FireF.bsp    : The map file. This goes in your fortress/maps directory.
-FireF.rmf    : Play with it in WorldCraft (you need to extract the textures though)
======================= Game Play ======================== 

This map is for 2 to 4 players.  Deathmatch style for Team Fortress.
Grenade 2 has been removed from the ammo packs.
