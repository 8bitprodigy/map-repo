======================= Installation ======================== 

This archive contains 2 files. 
 
-thechase.txt - Your reading it now.
-thechase.bsp -The map file. Place it with you other TeamFortress maps. 

======================= Game Play ======================== 
The is one flag in the yard between the two bases. Your team must get the flag
and cap it inside the enemy's base.
