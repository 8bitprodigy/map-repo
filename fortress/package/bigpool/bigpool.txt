Section 5 
======================= Game Play ======================== 

3 Teams, team 1 and 2 want team 3's Treasure Boxes, team 3 is there to spoil their plans
The treasure must be taken to the big green boxes on the platforms in the water.
If you shoot the light pole in either fort there is a resupply model of kenny he
will resupply Team 3 ONLY.

Team 1 and 2 are allowed 3 players each as scout, demo, medic, spy, pyro or engineer
Team 3 can have 6 players who can be either Snipers or Heavy Weapons Guys

There are Fish in the water and all the dogs belong to Team 3. (Mega-Team Fortress)

POINT SYSTEM:

1 treasure = 20 team points for Team 1 or 2 every 60 seconds possible
