===================Installation====================
The archive contains 2 files
          -lavatf.txt     This file
          -lavatf.bsp     The map file. Place it in \fortress\maps\ directory
====================Game=Play======================
This map is just like 2 forts style.

You take their flag and bring it up to your Sniper Nests and goto that thing sticking up
that looks like a teleport pad.
