* Play Information *

This map does not work properly without the TeamFortress QuakeC patch,
which can be downloaded from http://www.planetquake.com/teamfortress.

Go to your enemy's base, open their control room with the flashing
button and blow the crap outta the computer like boxes in there.
Your team gets 1 point per destruction, the scoring is limited to 1
point per second.
