Title                   : Iron Bunkers 2
Filename                : bunker2.bsp
Author                  : Dan "Hobbes" Rouse
Email Address           : hobbes_@softhome.net
Description             : Two strongholds of iron buried inside 
			  of rock.  Enter the forts through either
			  the front door, or the underwater caves.
			  After you've managed to get inside of 
			  enemy territory, grab their flag and 
			  make a run for it!  Bring the enemy flag 
			  to the capture pad in your ramp room.

How to use this map:
Copy the bunker2.bsp file into your quake\id1\maps or quake\fortress\maps
directory. Then run quake.
In the console, type "map bunker2" (without the quotes)
