Description: This is a take-off of the map sniperwar.  Many people did not 
	     play sniper war because they do not like sniping. 
	     Thats why I added the scouts in to let the other non-snipes
	     Have fun also. 


How to use this map:

Unzip g2snip.bsp to your quake\fortress\maps directory. Unzip flagtk.wav and flagcap.wav
in your quake\fortress\sound\misc directory. If you want to run it on a LAN server
or just take a peek at it, load quake with -game fortress and type
MAP g2snip in the console to load it.

If you want to play on a QW server running this map, copy the g2snip.bsp
file into your quake\fortress\maps directory and copy flagtk.wav and flagcap.wav into your
quake\fortress\sound\misc directory.
