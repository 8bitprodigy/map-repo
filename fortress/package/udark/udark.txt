 This archive contains 4 files.

	-backpack.mdl	This goes in your fortress/progs directory.
	-tf_flag.mdl	This goes in your fortress/progs directory.


Section 5 
======================= Game Play ======================== 

The objective of this map is to get onto the other teams base and capture the Flag in the 
basement.  Then return it to the top of your own base, near the sniper thingy.

to open the sniper area your team must complete one flag run, then after that get some more 
to score. :)

POINT SYSTEM:
10 points and 10 frags per capture
