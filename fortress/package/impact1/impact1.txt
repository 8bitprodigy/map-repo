* Version Updates *

This version (1.0.1) corrects two bugs:  the cave resupply backpacks 
now work for either team (though each team can only OPEN their own 
cave resupply), and the red camper-protection beams now function
properly.  Sorry about the trouble.

* Play Information *

This map does not work properly without the 
TeamFortress QuakeC patch, which can be downloaded from 
http://www.planetquake.com/teamfortress

* Game Play *

Standard 2forts style capture the flag... grab it from the center of the 
enemy base and take it to your battlements.  Quick, easy, simple.  Hope you
enjoy some of the outdoor parts.

Don't forget to put flagwisp.wav in /fortress/sound/items.
