Server info:  A fairly large map that should handle 32 people (max 16 per team). 
 Spam packs included.

General overview:  A 2fort-style map which blends a few features from several
of the more popular maps (popular with me, at any rate).  
The spam packs are "full-service", including resupply of 1 detpack.  
Bring the enemy flag back to your flag pedastle to cap.
