Section 5 
======================= Game Play ======================== 

The objective of this map is to controll the top of the hill. There are 4 forts and Each Team can only 
have 6 players. This is a very fast map and me and my cohorts have been enjoying it on the company LAN.



POINT SYSTEM:
Take the Hill and Score 10 for your Team every 30 Seconds
