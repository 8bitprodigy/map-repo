Section 5 
======================= Game Play ======================== 

The objective of this map is to capture the oponents flag while defending your own.
The flags are on top of each Castle. Take the flag to the chubby little boy on the back 
ramp of your castle to be rewarded. The back side of each castle has an entrance via the
slime moat.


POINT SYSTEM:
Cap flag for 10 Points and get resupplies for your team
