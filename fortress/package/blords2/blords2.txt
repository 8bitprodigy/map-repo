======================= Installation ======================== 

 This archive contains 4 files.

	-blords2.txt	You are reading it now.
	-blords.bsp	The map file. Place it with you other TeamFortress maps.
	-backpack.mdl	This goes in your fortress/progs directory.
	-moneybag.mdl	This goes in your fortress/progs directory.


Section 5 
======================= Game Play ======================== 

The objective of this map is to get onto the other teams fort and capture the bag of gold in the 
basement.  Then return it to the gold compactor in your own base, located oppisite the team flag
in the main room.


POINT SYSTEM:
10points and 10 frags per capture
