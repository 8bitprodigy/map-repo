* Play Information *

  This map does not work properly without the 
  TeamFortress QuakeC patch, which can be downloaded from 
  http://www.planetquake.com/teamfortress/
  or
  http://mordor.netlink.ch/games/tf/

  This is a capture-the-flag map designed for 2. The goal is to capture the flag, 
  that's located under water, *well you need to go underwater to go to the key room.
  of the teleporters to get back to the surface. And then run back to your base, and drop
  the flag at the dropoff point (which is located at the top of your base.).
