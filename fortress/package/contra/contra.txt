Details:
========
* Please Email me with any sugestions etc , Thanks.

This map is designed specifically for the TeamFortress quakec
patch v2.+
This is a two team capture-the-flag map.Each team has its own
Battleship.Infiltrate the enemy Battleship via the Transport-Shuttles,
and get their Flag.Afterwards return to your Ship and bring the flag
to your bridge.(The ring marks the Drop-off point)
For each touchdown the team gets 10 Points.

Where is the Touchdown position ?
