======================= Game Play ======================== 

This is a TeamFortress map designed for up to 16 players on 2 different
teams. This is a standard capture the flag style map. Take the opponents
flag and return it to your base.

There are 2 teams to this map. 

The Objective is to capture the enemy flag.

POINT SYSTEM:
Blue Team - 10 frags everytime someone on your team captures the flag
Red Team - 10 frags everytime someone on your team captures the flag
