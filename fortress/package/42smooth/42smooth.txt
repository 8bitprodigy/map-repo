* Changes to 32smooth*

-Removed quads
-Both former quad locations are now teleporters to battlements
-Flag room spawn moved to ramp room (backpack added there)
-Connected upper part of towers
-All doors open instantly and fully
-Higher ceilings in most areas
-More space to move in tight areas
-Replaced pointless lifts with ramps
-Flag return time reduced from 65 to 50 sec
-Need to press resupply buttons only once to fill up
-Made floor brown to avoid blue floor in red base (texture wall9_8 is now floor01_5)
-Fixed infamous misaligned buttons in red resupply
-Removed invisible teleporter textures in water
-Created a minimum light level throughout map
