* Description *

This is a TF map based on the Prez theme written by
members of the Evil Dead clan.  The map design by Death of
the Evil Dead and TF coding By Ludwig of the Evil Dead.

Four Teams
team1 - 2 Professors - Civilian
team2 - 4 Bodyguards - Scout, Soldier, Demoman, Medic, Pyro, Spy
team3 - 4 Assasins   - Sniper, Demoman, Spy
team4 - S.W.A.T.     - Sniper, Soldier, Demoman, Medic, Hwguy, Pyro, Engineer

*******Goals********

team1	Professor scores 20 points for making it to the Getaway Truck and
	30 for making it to the Getaway Chopper. 
	
team2	Bodyguards score 20 if Professor makes it to the Truck and 2 if he
	makes it to the Chopper.
	
team3	Assasins score 10 if Professor dies

team4	SWAT scores 10 if Professor makes it to Chopper and 2 if he makes
	it to the truck.
