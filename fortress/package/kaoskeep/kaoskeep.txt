======================= Installation ======================== 

This archive contains 2 files. 
 
-kaoskeep.txt - Your reading it now.
-kaoskeep.bsp -The map file. Place it with you other TeamFortress maps. 

======================= Game Play ======================== 
The object of this map is to capture the other teams flag and
return it your Goal. Simple eh. :)

Ten frags are awarded to the team the successfully return the 
enemy's flag to your tower.
