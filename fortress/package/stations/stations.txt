Details:

This is capture the flag style map, each team must steal the disk which is located
in the computer room in the lower level of the team's base and return it to
their own base where they can copy (capture) it.

If the carrier is killed, the disk is dropped and remains there for 30 seconds
until it is returned. It cannot be returned by team members running over it.
