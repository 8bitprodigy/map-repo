 ======================= Installation ======================== 

 This archive contains 3 files.


  -jailfort.txt : 
   You are reading it now.

  -jailfort.bsp : 
   The map file. Place it with you other TeamFortress maps.

  -reload.wav, taken1.wav, taken2.wav, flagcap.wav : 
   This goes in your fortress/sound/tfm/ directory.
