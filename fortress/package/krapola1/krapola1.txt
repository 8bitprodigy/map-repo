======================= Installation ======================== 

This archive contains 4 files. 
 
-krapola1.txt - Your reading it now.
-krapola1.bsp - The map file. Place it with you other TeamFortress maps. 
-backpack.mdl - This goes in your fortress/progs directory. 
-tf_flag.mdl  - This goes in your fortress/progs directory. 

======================= Game Play ======================== 

This is a TeamFortress "Krapola1" map.

There are two teams, the blue and the red. The blue team must capture
the red flag deep inside the red base and return it to there tank.

The red team must capture the blue flag deep inside the blue base and 
return it to there tank.
