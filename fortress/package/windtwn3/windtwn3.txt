Rule:
Get nuggets from gold mine. and touchdown to the bank in your town.

Nugget:
 Nugget exists at deepest area of gold mine.
 This is extremely heavy, So you can't walk / run quickly.
 If you find an enemy with a nugget, you can kill him and rob a nugget.

Bank:
 You can drop a nugget here.
 Bank is located near the town gate, so it can be attacked easily.
 You've better guard here :)

Gold Mine:
 Gate of mine is located in front of church.
 The switch of upper floor works for closing the mine for a while.
 You've better protect the switch from enemy.
 and, if you have any chances, can attack the enemy's switch.

Pentagram:
 You can reload health/armor/ ammo with touching the pentagram of your town,
 but you can't get invulnerability.
 There is 3 pentagram/town, can't use the pentagram of enemy's.

Backpack:
 Backpack is Located in front of church. You can reload only ammo with touching this.
 There's only one/town, both of team can use this.

Red armor & Mega health:
 There's 1/town, better remember.
