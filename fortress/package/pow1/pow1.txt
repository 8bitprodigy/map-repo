======================= Game Play ======================== 

This is a TeamFortress "Prisoner of war" map.

There are 3 teams to this map. The Army (red) who are trying to resque
the prisoner, the Prisoner waiting to be resqued (blue), and the 
Guerillas (Yellow) who are trying to stop the army from killing them and 
resquing the prisoner.

The army must resque the prisoner from the cage and escort him to the helipad. 
The guerillas must stop them from doing this.

POINT SYSTEM:
Army - 10 point for escorting the prisoner to the helipad.
Prisoner - 10 points for making it to the helipad.

Frags count in this game.
