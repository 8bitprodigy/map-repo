======================= Game Play ======================== 

This is a TeamFortress "3vs3" map.

There are 3 teams to this map. 
The blue team fights 3 on 3 with the red team, while the yellow team are spectators.

The Objective is to kill the opponent more than you die.

POINT SYSTEM:
Blue Team - 1 frag everytime you kill somebody
Red Team - 1 frag everytime you kill somebody
Yellow Team - No kills for you
