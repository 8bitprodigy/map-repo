 Q. How do I get to the Quad Damage?

 A. Any character class can get up the Quad by either rocket or grenade
    jumping over there. Naturally, the Soldier has an easy time doing this
    while a Scout has the hardest time doing it. If using a grenade, you have
    to play around with different angles until you can get it right. I did
    this on purpose because once a Scout has a Quad, he can kill any class
    fairly easily with his shotgun. Soldiers, on the other hand tend to gib
    themselves when rockets are this powerful, so it balances out.

 Q. How do I open the grates in the water?

 A. Use a detpack to blow them open; everyone sees a message letting them
    know when you have done so.

 Q. Will there be a JawRaw2?

 A. Yes, not only to fix any bugs but also to add new textures. If you
    have ideas or suggestions on any of this; feel free to e-mail us.
