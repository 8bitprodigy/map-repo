* Other Info *

There are two forts, underground.  Get to the enemy's fort, and work your 
way from the bottom to the top.  There is the flag.  Return it to 
the top of your own base.

Items are in a ring-shaped room around the central area


How to use this map:
Copy the vortex24.bsp file into your quake\id1\maps or quake\fortress\maps
directory. Then run quake.
In the console, type "map vortex24" (without the quotes)
