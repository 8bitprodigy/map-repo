* Other Info *
In the map Armageddon, you have to pick which god you wish to serve, Hera, the Queen of the
Gods, or Zeus, the King of the Gods.  Then you must defend there temple from pillagers and prevent the orb from 
being stolen.  And of course you must steal your opponents orb.


How to use this map:
Copy the armageddon.bsp file into your quake\fortress\maps directory. 

Included in Zip File:
A. Armageddon.bsp
B. Readme File
C. Mdl's (put those in quake\fortress\progs directory)
