Title                   : Quake Town
Filename                : qtown.bsp
Author                  : Bill Main (mmx1@mindspring.com)
Description             : The object of the game is to capture the money from the vault 
                          near the cops base by blowing the door using a detpack.  Then the
                          player must carry the gold to the room near the center of the 
			  map.
                          
================================================================
* Play Information *
