Section 5 
======================= Game Play ======================== 

This is a TeamFortress "Capture the Flag" map.

There are 2 teams to this map. (red) who is trying to cap the blue flag, 
(blue) who is trying to cap the red flag.

POINT SYSTEM:
blue- 10 frags everytime flag captured
red- 10 frags everytime flag captured
