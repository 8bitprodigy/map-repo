Details:

  This map is designed specifically for the TeamFortress quakec
patch v2+.

It is two team map, with the following objectives:
	
	-  Each team must enter the enemy, hit the 6 switches hidden around the base,
	and then enter the final door for victory.
	2 points is scored for each button pressed, with 100 points going to the team
	who gets through the final door.
